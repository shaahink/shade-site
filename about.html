Azarnoosh Hs is a fashion designer and artist from Tehran, now based in Gent, Belgium. Trained in fashion design at the University of Science and Culture (BA, 2021), she works across tailoring, costume, embroidery and print — from made-to-measure corsets and structured jackets to a hundred-hat project that toured Tehran’s independent fashion events.

These days she is a book designer & illustrator in residence at MER Books in Gent, where the shadows in her sketchbooks are finally getting speaking roles.
