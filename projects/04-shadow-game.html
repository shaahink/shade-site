The shadow game — an artist book

artist book · silkscreen & torn-paper collage

An illustrated artist book that follows a day narrated by a shadow. Silkscreened pages in cobalt, seafoam and flame red carry hand-torn scraps of text — “I was the one”, “that was me”, “I was the darkness watching you” — as the story slips between the page and whoever is holding it.
