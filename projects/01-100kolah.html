100Kolah — the hat project

2024 – 2025 · Tehran · costumed & ready-to-wear hats

Kolah means hat in Farsi — and 100Kolah set out to make a hundred of them, each with its own personality: dripping brims, strawberry seeds, rose-petal linings and the occasional tower of five hats worn at once. The project made the rounds of Tehran’s independent fashion scene, showing up (on heads) at Balkon Fashion Event, The Rakht and Koocheh Music Festival.
