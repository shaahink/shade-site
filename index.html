Work

They call her Shade — سایه.

Fashion designer and artist, from Tehran, based in Gent. She works across tailoring, costume, embroidery, silkscreen and artist books — corsets and structured jackets made to measure, a hundred-hat project, drawings in red thread, and books where the shadow does the talking. Currently designing books at MER, Gent.
